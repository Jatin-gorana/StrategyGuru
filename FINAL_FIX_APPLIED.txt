WHAT WAS WRONG
================================================================================

1. yfinance is Unreliable
   - Yahoo Finance blocks requests with 429 errors
   - No fallback mechanism
   - Retries take 60+ seconds

2. Previous Implementation Was Flawed
   - Tried yfinance 5 times before falling back
   - Each retry took 20+ seconds
   - Total timeout exceeded 60 seconds
   - User had to wait forever

3. Timeout Issues
   - 60 second timeout was being exceeded
   - System was waiting for yfinance to fail repeatedly
   - Poor user experience
